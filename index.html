What is my Hobby?: Suturing

By: Isaac Nieto Period 1

Volunteering at a Hospital

I volunteer at San Antonio Regional Hospital. I work in the Emergency Department doing many things. I suture in my past time to gain experience in my future field of medicine. I am going to become a general surgeon, this requires the use of sutures to patch up patients with open wounds.

Why do I suture?

I suture because to me, it allows me to be more versatile in dangerous situations. As a doctor, you need to be ready to assist at any time. So being able to suture well, will benefit myself in the long run.
